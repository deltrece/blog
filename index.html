May is Bike Month

May 1, 2023

It's Spring and that means warmer weather and the opportunity to be outdoors more frequently. People, families and even pets will be outdoors more and some will even be outdoors on their bicycle. Cycling is a great way to release stress, reduce your carbon footprint and increase your fitness activities. Making sure that your bike is ride ready (A-B-C-Quick Check) will ensure that you are riding safe and that you can have an enjoyable experience. Learn bicycle safety tips and discover more about National Bike Month at the Bike League's, National Bike Month's page for education and search for an event near you.

National Foster Care Month

May 1, 2023

I recently came to the realization that I don't have many photos of myself during junior high and high school years. Those years I was in the foster care system and capturing moments and documenting memories were the furthest from my mind and also from the mind of many of my foster caregivers. This photo may be from 11th or 12th grade and I may have just finished watching the Malcolm X movie, lol. I don't remember honestly, however I do remember that it was after watching this movie that led to my decision to stop eating pork cold turkey. Haven't looked back since.
