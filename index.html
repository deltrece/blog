Vegan Polish Boy

February 15, 2023

How do I tell you I'm from Cleveland without telling you I'm from Cleveland? With a polish boy of course! Although I eat vegan meals, doesn't mean I eat salads all day. Comfort foods are so delicious and recreating them vegan style is a favorite activity. To make this polish boy, I cook a Beyond Meats Plant Based Link Original in the air fryer, lightly toast a hoagie bun, whip up some homemade coleslaw, add frech fries and bbq sauce. I make two and eat two of them, because it's good.
